This paper investigates the perception bottleneck that limits vision-language models' ability to understand charts effectively. We analyze the challenges and propose solutions for improving VLM performance on chart-related tasks.

**Key Contributions:**
- Identification of perception bottleneck in VLMs for chart understanding
- Comprehensive analysis of VLM limitations on chart data
- effective methods for enhancing VLM chart comprehension
