This work explores the fundamental challenges that Vision-Language Models (VLMs) face when understanding and interpreting charts. We identify key perception bottlenecks that limit current models' ability to accurately extract and reason about visual information in charts.

**Key Contributions:**
- Systematic analysis of VLM perception limitations for charts
- Identification of critical bottlenecks in chart understanding
- Novel evaluation framework for chart comprehension
