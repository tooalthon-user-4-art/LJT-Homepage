C-Eval is a comprehensive evaluation suite designed to assess the capabilities of foundation models across multiple disciplines and difficulty levels in Chinese. It provides a standardized benchmark for evaluating model performance on diverse tasks.

**Key Contributions:**
- Multi-level difficulty evaluation framework
- Multi-discipline coverage (humanities, STEM, social sciences, etc.)
- Comprehensive benchmark for Chinese foundation models
