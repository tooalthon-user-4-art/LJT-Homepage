# About Me

I am a **first-year PhD candidate** at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://hkust-nlp.github.io/), working under the supervision of [Professor Junxian He](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~jxhe/). My research focuses on **natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning**, with particular interests in:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
